Kill the Life at NNlk05's website.

Welcome to Kill the Life! Click the button below to start the game.

In this game, you will be presented with a grid of cells.
Some cells will be alive, and some will be dead.
The cells follow these rules:
A dead cell becomes live if it is surrounded by exactly three live neighbours, and otherwise remains dead;
A live cell survives if it is surrounded by two or three live neighbours, and otherwise dies.
Your goal is to eliminate all the live cells by clicking on them.
Try to kill the life in as few clicks as possible!
